The Payment Journey

Every payment follows this core 7-step lifecycle. Each module explores parts of this journey.

01. Initiation

User pushes/pulls money (Mod 2, 3).

02. Transportation

Messages route across networks (Mod 1, 3).

03. Authorization

Issuer approves ledger debit (Mod 3).

04. Settlement

Reserves settle at Federal Reserve (Mod 1).

05. Risk Management

Mitigating return rates and declines (Mod 2, 3, 4, 5).

06. Regulation

Enforcing rules: Reg E, Durbin (All Mod).

07. Economics

Fee splits, interchange splits (Mod 2, 3, 4).
